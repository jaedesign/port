ABOUT

JASON ELLIOTT

Jason Elliott, is a Graphic Designer and Illustrator, currently attending the Conestoga College Graphic Design program. He has 4 years of experience, perfecting and expanding his skill-set through the practice of Graphic Design and Illustration.

From a young age, he has been interested in technology related solutions, such as Graphic Design, Web-Design, Coding, Animation and Illustration. He has always been a creative individual, interacting with online communities to further expand his knowledge, whether it be learning a new coding language or design software, he has consistently been in search of finding the next level of creative fulfillment.

Being fluent with the industry standard Graphic Design tools throughout the Adobe Creative Suite, he is capable of creating web-design, branding, logo design, typographic and illustrative solutions. Over the years, Jason has engaged with clients on a personal level, to bring their creative visions to life. Currently, Jason is an aspiring 2D and 3D illustrator and Graphic Designer, taking on a variety of creative projects. He has worked on several freelance projects.
