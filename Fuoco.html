Fuoco - Premium Frozen Pizza

Premium Frozen Pizza Packaging Design

Fuoco is a conceptual pizza company that specializes in premium consumer pizzas, that are notably “tastier than takeout.” Throughout my research I found that some of the most premium branded pizzas used darker colours, such as gray and black, often with some use of a contrasting colour. The design for Fuoco creates an instant premium look and feel, different than anything on the market, additionaly looking expensive and “healthier.” The logo takes a traditional pizza approach, although modernized and suiting for the Fuoco brand. The design uses interchangeable colours throughout the blend of flames, and changes depending on the flavour.
