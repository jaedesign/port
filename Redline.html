Redline

Restaurant Brand Guide

Redline is a conceptual restaurant, based around the themes of classic racing and Nascar. Redline is targeted to those that are automotive enthusiasts, and those simply in-search of an excellent and thrilling dining experience, containing scenery and colours often used throughout racing. The name “Redline” is a standard term using in racing, in which RPM of the car is at full throttle.

The colour red is known to be sporty, and is often used throughout racing, and the automotive industry. Red is also known to bring excitement and energy, which racing often inflicts. The fork element within the logo distinguishes, and sets Redline apart from being an automotive company, and is immediately recognized as a restaurant. The branding across Redline easily allows customers to have a grasp of the restaurants environment, through the visuals alone.

The typeface used across the Redline branding, uses a slight slant, which creates movement throughout the design. The word-mark also uses elements to create individuality, including the stroke in the word “line” developing another subtle, although important aspect to the Redline brand.

The logo created for Redline is applicable at any scale with very few restrictions or quality loss, creating a seamless and memorable icon. This logo immediately creates brand recognition directly to the consumer, and offers a wide variety of use cases, instantly establishing and setting the tone of the Redline environment.
