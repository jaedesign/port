LUNA

ORGANIC SOAP

Luna is a company that specializes in organic soaps and CBD products, creating a sensual experience to help consumers relax, providing many natural health benefits to the skin.

This product  contains CBD infused products, which have been proven to provide hydration, pain relief and improved skin. The product design for Luna was inspired by the euphoric and relaxing feel of the mountains, along with the colour palette, creating a romantic, calming and “moody” scenery.

Luna stimulates the feeling of desire, as it opens the doors to an extremely pleasant and peaceful experience, potentially improving the consumers quality of life. Initially the product design had started off very abstract, and later became more scenic, creating a beautiful and desirable atmosphere.
