Hill and Sons

Brewery Brand Guide

Hill and Sons is a conceptual brewery targeted towards those that are interested in extreme sports, graffiti styled artwork, and those that are simply in search for great tasting and refreshing alcoholic beverages.

Hill and Sons targets the younger, legal drinking ages, with the intent to introduce a new audience to craft beer. Whilst conceptualizing for Hill and Sons, the name “Hill and Sons” was envisioned quite figuratively. The “Sons” are essentially raised by the “Hills,” creating the dynamic of extreme sports. Over time, the Sons became rebellious vandals, and began to cause mayhem, although continued to be passionate about their beloved extreme sports. Hill and Sons creates an outlet for those who are rebellious, although ambitious, and encourages consumers to fulfill their passions.

The logo for “Hill and Sons” establishes an instantly recognizable, and legible icon. The logo seamlessly forms the “H” and the “S” together, consisting of several variations that can be used under different circumstances.

Hill and Sons uses elements from street-art and graffiti to immediately create a sense of character within the cans. The can designs use bright colours, which instantly creates consumer recognition, and adds shelf value.

The main objective of this brand identity for Hill and Sons was to create a brewery that focused heavily on the experience of participating in extreme sports, also with the intent of promoting ambition, dedication and rebellion, whilst introudcing a younger, drinking age audience to the craft beer scene.
