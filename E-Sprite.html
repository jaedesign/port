E-SPRITE

E-SPRITE is an electric bike trading company and is the home to three of the best, new electric bicycle models. Our store is the place to visit if you are wanting to experience adventure, liveliness and a rush of freedom and life.

We not only focus on the environment and becoming more eco-friendly, but we are also a great business that helps with the current living situation we are all currently experiencing, with the coronavirus. Being stuck at home and having to worry about social distancing can be tough, although having an e-bike is an awesome way to fix that trapped feeling that can often consume us, while being stuck at home. E-Sprite targets people who not only enjoy the outdoors, adventures, exercising, nature but also those that are environmentally friendly and cautious.

If this fits you then you are in the right place! E-Sprite is also a great place for families, friends and bike groups to shop at. Biking with E-Sprite is a great way to get outside and stay active, while also being cautious of our environment, carbon footprint and global warming.

 E-SPRITE comes from the actual meaning of the word Spright, meaning to be sprightly, full of spirit, happy, positive attitudes and someone who radiates positive energy

These are the characteristics you'll surely experience after purchasing from E-SPRITE, and riding one of our amazing bikes.
